The Spirit of Jalisco

Tequila Lounge was founded in 2018 by Carlos Mendoza, a third-generation tequila maker from Jalisco, Mexico, and Sofia Rodriguez, an award-winning mixologist. Their vision was to create a space that celebrates the rich heritage of tequila while introducing innovative cocktails that showcase this versatile spirit.

Our bar features authentic décor sourced directly from Mexico, including hand-painted tiles, traditional artwork, and vintage tequila artifacts that tell the story of this iconic spirit. The warm, vibrant atmosphere is designed to transport you to the heart of Mexico, whether you're a tequila connoisseur or just beginning your journey.

We take pride in our carefully curated selection of tequilas and mezcals, each chosen for its quality, authenticity, and unique character. Our team regularly visits distilleries across Mexico to discover new and exceptional spirits to share with our guests. We believe in supporting traditional production methods and small-batch producers who maintain the highest standards of craftsmanship.

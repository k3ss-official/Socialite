Our Story

Discover the history and inspiration behind our authentic Prohibition-era speakeasy.

A Tribute to the Roaring Twenties

The Speakeasy was founded in 2018 by cocktail historian and mixologist James Sullivan, whose fascination with Prohibition-era drinking culture inspired him to create an authentic experience for modern patrons. Housed in a historic building that once served as an actual speakeasy during the 1920s, our establishment pays homage to this rich history while offering contemporary comforts.

Every detail of our space has been meticulously designed to transport guests back to the Roaring Twenties. From the vintage furniture and Art Deco accents to the period-appropriate lighting and music, we've created an immersive environment that captures the allure and excitement of a bygone era. Even our staff dress in period-appropriate attire, enhancing the authentic speakeasy experience.

Our cocktail program is the heart of The Speakeasy, featuring both historically accurate recipes from the Prohibition era and creative interpretations that honor the spirit of innovation that characterized the underground bar scene of the 1920s. We use traditional techniques, premium spirits, and house-made ingredients to craft drinks that would make even the most discerning flapper or gangster proud.

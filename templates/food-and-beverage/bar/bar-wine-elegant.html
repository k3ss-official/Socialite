Our Story

Discover the passion and expertise behind Vineyard Lounge, where wine appreciation meets elegant hospitality.

A Sanctuary for Wine Lovers

Founded in 2018 by sommelier Claire Dubois and restaurateur Michael Chen, Vineyard Lounge was born from a shared passion for exceptional wines and a desire to create a space where both connoisseurs and curious newcomers could explore the world of wine in a welcoming environment.

Our wine bar features an ever-evolving selection of wines from established estates and emerging vineyards alike. We believe that every bottle tells a story—of terroir, tradition, and craftsmanship—and we're dedicated to sharing these stories with our guests.

The interior of Vineyard Lounge was designed to create an atmosphere of refined comfort, with warm lighting, natural materials, and intimate seating arrangements that encourage conversation and connection.

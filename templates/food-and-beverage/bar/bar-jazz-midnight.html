Our Story

A Tribute to Jazz

Midnight Lounge was born from a deep love of jazz and craft cocktails. Founded in 2018 by James Wilson, a former jazz pianist, and Elena Rodriguez, an award-winning mixologist, our bar combines their passions into a unique experience.

Our space was once a historic recording studio where legendary jazz artists created timeless music in the 1950s. We've preserved many original elements while adding modern touches to create an atmosphere that honors the past while embracing the present.

At Midnight Lounge, we believe that great music deserves great drinks. Our cocktail program draws inspiration from jazz eras and iconic musicians, creating flavor profiles that complement the sounds filling our space. Every detail, from the lighting to the acoustics, has been carefully considered to enhance your experience.

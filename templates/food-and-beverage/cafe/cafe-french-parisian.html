A Slice of Paris

Café Parisien was born from a deep love of French culinary traditions and café culture. Founded in 2015 by Chef Pierre Dubois, who trained at prestigious patisseries in Paris, our café brings authentic French flavors and ambiance to your neighborhood.

After years of perfecting his craft in France, Chef Pierre returned with a vision to create a space where people could experience the charm of a Parisian café without leaving the city. Every detail, from our hand-painted tiles to the wicker chairs on our terrace, has been carefully selected to transport you to the streets of Paris.

We believe in preserving traditional French baking techniques while sourcing the finest local and imported ingredients. Our pastries are made fresh daily in our kitchen, and our coffee beans are selected from sustainable farms and roasted to highlight their unique characteristics.

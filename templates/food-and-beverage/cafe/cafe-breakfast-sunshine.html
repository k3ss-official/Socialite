Our Story

Discover how Café Sunshine became the neighborhood's favorite breakfast and brunch destination.

A Bright Beginning

Café Sunshine was founded in 2018 by Sarah and Michael Thompson, a husband-and-wife team with a passion for great food and warm hospitality. After years of working in various restaurants and cafés, they dreamed of creating a space where people could start their day with delicious food, exceptional coffee, and a genuinely cheerful atmosphere.

The name "Sunshine" was inspired by Sarah's grandmother, who always said that breakfast should be as bright and uplifting as the morning sun. This philosophy guides everything we do, from our sunny yellow decor to our focus on fresh, vibrant ingredients that energize and delight.

What began as a small, 20-seat café has grown into a beloved community hub, where neighbors meet for morning catch-ups, friends gather for weekend brunches, and families create special memories together. We've expanded our space and menu over the years, but our commitment to quality, warmth, and that special "sunshine" feeling remains unchanged.

Our Story

Discover how Gastro Pub is redefining the British pub experience with exceptional food, drink, and hospitality.

Elevating Pub Tradition

Gastro Pub was founded in 2018 by Chef James Wilson and restaurateur Sarah Mitchell, who shared a vision of creating a space that honored the convivial spirit of the traditional British pub while elevating the food and drink experience to new heights.

Housed in a beautifully restored Victorian building, our gastropub combines original architectural features with contemporary design elements to create a space that feels both timeless and modern. The warm, inviting atmosphere encourages lingering over good food and conversation, just as pubs have done for centuries.

Our culinary approach is rooted in British tradition but not bound by it. Chef Wilson and his team take classic pub dishes and reimagine them with refined techniques, seasonal ingredients, and creative presentation. We work closely with local farmers, fishermen, and artisanal producers to source the finest ingredients, many of which are highlighted on our menu.

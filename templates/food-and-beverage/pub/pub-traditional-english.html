Our Story

Discover the rich history and tradition behind Rustic Barrel, a cornerstone of our community for generations.

A Heritage of Hospitality

Established in 1842, Rustic Barrel has been serving the community for over 180 years. Our pub began as a humble alehouse, providing respite for local workers and travelers alike.

Over the generations, we've maintained our commitment to traditional brewing methods, classic pub fare, and the warm, welcoming atmosphere that has made British pubs the heart of their communities for centuries.

Today, we continue to honor our heritage while embracing modern tastes, offering a perfect blend of tradition and contemporary comfort.

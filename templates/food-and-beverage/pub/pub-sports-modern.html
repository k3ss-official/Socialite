Our Story

From passionate sports fans to craft beer enthusiasts, discover how Hoppy Hour became the ultimate game day destination.

Born From Passion

Hoppy Hour was founded in 2012 by former college athletes and longtime friends, Mike and Dave. After years of struggling to find the perfect place to watch games with great beer and food, they decided to create it themselves.

What started as a small neighborhood bar with just a few TVs has grown into the premier sports viewing destination in the city. We've expanded our space, added our outdoor beer garden, and continuously refined our craft beer selection and menu offerings.

Our mission remains the same: to create the ultimate gathering place for sports fans and beer enthusiasts alike, where everyone feels welcome and the energy on game day is unmatched.

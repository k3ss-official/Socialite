Our Story

Discover the history and passion behind Pub & Grub, your authentic Irish pub experience.

A Taste of Ireland

Pub & Grub was established in 2010 by the O'Sullivan family, who brought their Irish heritage and passion for authentic pub culture to our community. With deep roots in County Cork, Ireland, the O'Sullivans created a space that captures the essence of a traditional Irish pub while embracing the local community.

Our pub features authentic d√©cor imported directly from Ireland, including vintage signage, traditional wooden furnishings, and memorabilia that tells the story of Irish culture and history. The warm, inviting atmosphere is designed to make everyone feel welcome, whether you're a regular or a first-time visitor.

We take pride in our commitment to authenticity, from our carefully curated selection of Irish beers and whiskeys to our traditional recipes passed down through generations. Our staff embodies the famous Irish hospitality, ensuring that every visit to Pub & Grub is memorable and enjoyable.

OUR STORY

Discover the passion behind our brewery and pub.

Crafting Great Beer Since 2010

Hopworks began as a passion project between two friends, Mike Reynolds and Sarah Chen, who shared a love for great beer and a vision to create a community gathering place centered around craft brewing. After years of home brewing and working in various breweries across the country, they decided to combine their expertise and open their own brewery pub in 2010.

What started with just three signature beers and a small pub menu has grown into a beloved local institution with a 15-barrel brewing system producing over 20 different craft beers throughout the year. Our commitment to quality ingredients, traditional brewing methods, and innovative flavor profiles has earned us numerous awards and a dedicated following of beer enthusiasts.

Today, Hopworks is not just a place to enjoy exceptional beer and foodâ€”it's a community hub where people gather to celebrate, learn about brewing, and share in the craft beer experience. Our brewery tours, tasting events, and beer education programs reflect our passion for sharing the art and science of brewing with our community.

OUR CRAFT BEERS

Explore our selection of handcrafted beers, brewed on-site with the finest ingredients and traditional methods.

Hopworks IPA

Our flagship IPA features a bold blend of Cascade, Centennial, and Simcoe hops, creating a citrusy, piney profile with a balanced malt backbone.

6.8% ABV Flagship

Amber Horizon

A smooth, malty amber ale with notes of caramel, toffee, and a subtle hop finish. Perfect for those who prefer a balanced, less hoppy beer.

5.5% ABV Smooth

Midnight Porter

A robust porter with rich flavors of dark chocolate, coffee, and roasted malt, finishing with a subtle smokiness and velvety mouthfeel.

6.2% ABV Rich

Citrus Wheat

A refreshing wheat beer infused with orange peel and coriander, creating a bright, citrusy flavor profile perfect for warm weather.

5.0% ABV Refreshing

Double Hop Imperial IPA

A bold, hop-forward Imperial IPA featuring intense citrus and tropical fruit notes with a strong malt backbone to balance the high IBUs.

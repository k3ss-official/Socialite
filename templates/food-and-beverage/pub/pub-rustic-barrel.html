Our Story

Discover how The Rustic Barrel became the neighborhood's favorite craft beer destination.

Crafted with Passion

The Rustic Barrel was founded in 2018 by longtime friends and beer enthusiasts, Jack Thompson and Mike Reynolds. After years of home brewing and exploring craft breweries across the country, they dreamed of creating a space where people could discover exceptional beers in a welcoming, unpretentious environment.

Our pub is housed in a renovated barrel factory from the 1920s, preserving much of the original architecture and incorporating reclaimed materials throughout the space. The exposed brick walls, wooden beams, and vintage brewing equipment create an atmosphere that honors the craft and tradition of beer making.

What began as a small taproom with just 8 beers has grown into a beloved community hub, featuring over 20 rotating taps, a full kitchen serving elevated pub fare, and a spacious beer garden. We've built relationships with local brewers, farmers, and artisans to create an experience that celebrates craftsmanship and community.

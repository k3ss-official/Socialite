Our Story

Discover the heart and history behind Rustic Table, where family traditions and delicious food come together.

A Family Tradition

Rustic Table began in 1985 when the Johnson family decided to share their beloved family recipes with the community. What started as a small diner has grown into a beloved local institution, but our commitment to homemade quality and warm hospitality has never changed.

Now in its second generation of family ownership, we continue to serve the comfort food classics that have made us famous, while also introducing new dishes that reflect our community's evolving tastes.

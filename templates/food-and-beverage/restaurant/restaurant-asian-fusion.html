A Culinary Journey

Umami Fusion was founded in 2020 by Chef David Kim, whose culinary journey spans over 15 years across prestigious kitchens in Tokyo, Seoul, Hong Kong, and Bangkok. After mastering traditional techniques in each cuisine, Chef Kim envisioned a restaurant that would celebrate the diversity of Asian flavors while breaking down cultural boundaries.

Our restaurant's design reflects our culinary philosophyâ€”a harmonious blend of traditional and contemporary elements. The sleek, minimalist interior features natural materials, subtle Asian-inspired accents, and an open kitchen that invites guests to witness the artistry behind each dish.

At Umami Fusion, we believe that food is a universal language that brings people together. Our menu tells a story of cultural exchange and innovation, with each dish representing a unique dialogue between different Asian culinary traditions. We're committed to honoring these traditions while pushing boundaries to create something truly distinctive.

Meet Our Chef

The culinary vision behind Umami Fusion's innovative Asian cuisine.

Chef David Kim

Born in Seoul and raised in a family of restaurateurs, Chef David Kim developed a passion for cooking at an early age. After graduating from the Culinary Institute of America, he embarked on a journey across Asia, working in Michelin-starred restaurants in Tokyo, Hong Kong, and Bangkok.

During his travels, Chef Kim became fascinated by the common threads that connect different Asian cuisinesâ€”the balance of flavors, the respect for ingredients, and the emphasis on seasonality. This fascination evolved into a vision for a restaurant that would celebrate these connections while creating something entirely new.

At Umami Fusion, Chef Kim combines traditional Asian techniques with modern culinary innovations to create dishes that honor their cultural origins while pushing boundaries. His approach is characterized by meticulous attention to detail, creative presentations, and an unwavering commitment to authentic flavors.

"My goal is to create a dialogue between different culinary traditions," says Chef Kim. "Each dish tells a story of cultural exchange and innovation, inviting guests to experience Asian cuisine in a new and exciting way."

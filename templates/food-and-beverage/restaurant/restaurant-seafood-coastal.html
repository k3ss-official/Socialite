Our Story

Discover the passion behind Ocean Harvest.

A Tradition of Excellence Since 2005

Ocean Harvest was founded by Chef James Mitchell, whose family has been in the fishing industry for generations. Growing up on the coast, James developed a deep appreciation for the ocean and its bounty. After training at prestigious culinary schools and working in renowned seafood restaurants around the world, he returned to his hometown to establish a restaurant that would honor the sea and its treasures.

What began as a small seafood shack has evolved into a premier dining destination, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and culinary innovation. Our waterfront location provides a stunning backdrop for enjoying the freshest seafood, caught daily by local fishermen with whom we've built strong relationships over the years.

Today, Ocean Harvest continues to be guided by our founding principles: respect for the ocean, support for our local fishing community, and a dedication to providing our guests with an exceptional dining experience that celebrates the flavors of the sea.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Learn about our efforts to protect the ocean and support sustainable fishing practices.

Protecting Our Oceans

At Ocean Harvest, sustainability isn't just a buzzword—it's at the core of everything we do. We believe that responsible sourcing and environmental stewardship are essential to preserving our oceans for future generations.

Sustainable Sourcing

We partner exclusively with fishermen who follow sustainable fishing practices. All our seafood is traceable to its source, and we only serve species that are not overfished or harvested in ways that harm marine ecosystems.

Zero Waste Initiative

We're committed to minimizing waste through composting, recycling, and using biodegradable packaging. Our goal is to achieve zero waste to landfill by utilizing every part of the seafood we source.

Community Support

We support local fishing communities through fair pricing and long-term relationships. A portion of our profits goes to ocean conservation efforts and educational programs about sustainable seafood.

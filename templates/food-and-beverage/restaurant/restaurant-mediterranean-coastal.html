A Mediterranean Journey

Bistro Méditerranée was born from Chef Maria Rossi's travels throughout the Mediterranean region. After years of studying under renowned chefs in Greece, Italy, Spain, and Lebanon, she returned home with a vision to create a dining experience that celebrates the diverse yet interconnected culinary traditions of the Mediterranean.

Opened in 2018, our restaurant quickly became known for its authentic flavors, warm hospitality, and commitment to quality. We believe in honoring traditional cooking methods while incorporating contemporary techniques to enhance the dining experience.

Our space was designed to evoke the feeling of a seaside Mediterranean bistro, with azure blue accents, natural wood, and terracotta elements creating a warm, inviting atmosphere. Whether you're enjoying a romantic dinner, family celebration, or casual lunch, we aim to transport you to the sun-drenched coasts of the Mediterranean.

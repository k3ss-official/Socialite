Our Story

Discover the tradition and excellence behind Steakhouse 1885.

A Legacy of Excellence

Steakhouse 1885 was founded by master butcher James Harrington, whose family had been in the meat business for generations. Named for the year the Harrington family first opened their butcher shop, our restaurant continues a long tradition of expertise in selecting and preparing the finest cuts of meat.

Our restaurant is housed in a beautifully restored historic building that dates back to the early 1900s. We've preserved many of the original architectural elements, including exposed brick walls, hardwood floors, and soaring ceilings, while adding modern touches to create an atmosphere of timeless elegance.

What sets us apart is our commitment to the art of dry-aging. Our custom-built aging room allows us to control temperature and humidity precisely, creating the perfect environment for developing the complex flavors and tenderness that make our steaks exceptional. We source our beef from select farms that share our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical practices.
